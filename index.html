About
Who I am and how I work

I'm Tegan. I'm a product and operations leader with six years of hands-on experience building, scaling and delivering across some pretty different problem spaces: social agencies, AI platforms, film festivals, sustainability brands and SaaS products. What's stayed consistent across all of it is the same core skillset. I build the systems and processes that let teams move fast without losing quality. I coordinate across engineering, design, marketing and leadership to keep work on track. And I figure out what needs to happen next when nobody has written the playbook yet.

I started by building Yolk Media from scratch, which meant being the strategist, the project manager, the creative director and the account lead all at once across 10+ concurrent client accounts. That taught me how to operate under pressure, manage competing priorities without dropping the ball, and build operational infrastructure that actually holds up over time. Three core clients stayed for all four years, which in an agency environment says something.

That foundation translated directly into product. I led two AI platforms through the full product lifecycle, from user research and roadmap planning through to launch and post-launch iteration, coordinating cross-functional teams and executive stakeholders with a 92% on-time delivery rate and around AUD 200K per month in revenue across both products.

Founding something teaches you things you can't learn any other way. I know how to move fast without losing quality, how to hold a vision together across a team that doesn't always agree, and how to make decisions when there's no playbook and the clock is running. Now I'm taking all of that into an environment with the scale, the talent and the ambition to match it.
