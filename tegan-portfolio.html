About Me

I'm Tegan.
I figure it out.


I lead AI products from concept to launch, coordinating engineers, designers, marketers and directors, and delivering a 92% on-time rate across two builds now generating around AUD 200K per month. Before that I spent four years running Yolk Media, a social media agency I built from scratch, which in practice meant being the strategist, the project manager, the creative director and the account lead all at once.

I've worked across a lot of different problem spaces: film festivals, SaaS platforms, sustainability brands, AI tooling. What's stayed consistent is the part of the job I'm drawn to most: taking something ambiguous, building the structure it needs, and getting it shipped.

Founding something teaches you things you can't learn any other way. I know how to move fast without losing quality, how to hold a vision together across a team that doesn't always agree, and how to make decisions when there's no playbook and the clock is running. Now I'm taking that to the next level, bringing everything I've built into an environment with the scale, the talent and the ambition to match it.
